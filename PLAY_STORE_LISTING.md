## 🇬🇧 English

### Short description (max 80 chars)
Track expenses, income & budgets — smart and simple.

### Long description (max 4000 chars)
**V&M Balance** is a powerful personal finance management app that helps you track expenses, income, and budgets all in one place.

**Key Features:**

💰 **Transaction Tracking**
Add income and expenses quickly and easily. Scan receipts with your camera for automatic data entry powered by AI.

📊 **Budgets & Planning**
Create category budgets and track how much you've spent against your plan. Share budgets with family or a partner.

💳 **Multiple Payment Sources**
Manage cash, bank accounts, cards, and digital wallets. Track the balance of each source separately.

📁 **Projects & Expenses**
Organize expenses by projects. Perfect for renovations, trips, or business projects with milestone tracking.

🔄 **Recurring Transactions**
Set up automatic reminders for rent, subscriptions, and regular expenses.

🤖 **AI Financial Assistant**
Ask questions about your finances and get smart insights and personalized advice.

📈 **Reports & Analytics**
Detailed charts and reports showing where your money goes. Export data to PDF or CSV.

🔒 **Privacy & Security**
Your data is protected and never shared with third parties.

👥 **Member Sharing**
Invite family members or colleagues for joint management of budgets and projects.
